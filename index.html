Selected Projects

[Research Projects]

Intelligent Mass Spectrometry Prediction System based on Autoregressive Graph Transformers (AC-AR-GT)

Shihao Guo
Research Assistant, AIMS Lab, HKUST-GZ | Dec 2025 – Mar 2026

Proposed AC-AR-GT, a novel system integrating autoregressive Graph Transformers with bond dissociation energy features for mass spectrometry prediction. Constructed a molecular graph encoder using Graph Transformers, incorporated historical fragment embedding via AdaLN for unified modeling of molecule-fragment interactions. Optimized with PPO reinforcement learning to navigate massive action spaces, achieving superior predictive performance over traditional static models.

SemiDis: Semi-supervised Learning Algorithm with Adaptive Temperature

Shihao Guo
Research Assistant, AIMS Lab, HKUST-GZ | Dec 2025 – Mar 2026

Addressed the limitations of FixMatch semi-supervised algorithm's global fixed confidence threshold. Proposed SemiDis by designing sample-wise reliability scores r(x) and dynamic adaptive temperature T(x) to convert reliability into probability thresholds. Introduced KL divergence based knowledge distillation loss to transfer distribution information from pseudo-labels. Implemented with USB framework supporting DDP distributed training.

AgentGR: Multi-agent Group Recommendation System

Co-authors, Shihao Guo (Student 2nd Author)
Submitted to SIGIR 2026 (CCF A, Under Review) | Nov 2024 – Present | Xidian ISET Lab

Proposed a Multi-agent framework to address data sparsity in group recommendation. Each group member is simulated by an LLM-driven Agent that reasons with personal preferences, then integrates via collaboration–negotiation mechanisms. Designed semantic meta-path preference reasoning, Leader-aware decision strategies, and robust training with multi-source noise to model dynamic group interactions, improving NDCG and HitRate by over 10%.

MA4CR: Multi-Agent Collaborative Online Course Recommendation Framework

Shihao Guo (Student 1st Author)
Submitted to Acta Automatica Sinica (CCF T1) | Nov 2024 – Present | Xidian ISET Lab

Proposed MA4CR, a recommendation framework built with AutoGen to simulate learners' cognitive processes through collaborative agents. Designed cognitive state and learning preference memory mechanisms, integrated with a long/short-term dual-strategy recommendation model, enabling precise knowledge tracing and personalized course delivery. This project won the "Challenge Cup" National Grand Prize.

VLM-RL Collaborative Framework for Autonomous Driving

Shihao Guo
Research Project | Feb 2025 – May 2025

Designed a collaborative framework using Visual Language Models (VLM) to generate semantic rewards based on Contrastive Language Goals (CLG). Optimized the policy using Soft Actor-Critic (SAC) with batched experience replay. Improved Average Speed (AS) and Route Completion (RC) by over 8% compared to existing methods.

RL-based Intelligent Image Cache Model

Shihao Guo
AI Engineer Intern, Huawei 2012 Labs | Aug 2025 – Dec 2025
